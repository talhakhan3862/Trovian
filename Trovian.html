About the Practice

Established in 2022, Trovian by Archi Trove is the design practice of Talha Khan. We focus on cultural architecture, interior spaces, and experimental concepts. Our approach bridges the gap between strong conceptual frameworks and buildable reality, always prioritizing context and clarity.

Design Philosophy

  • Context-Driven

    Responsive architectural solutions that respect their environment and climate.

  • Narrative Based

    Cultural storytelling woven intricately into the built form.

  • Landscape Integration

    Blurring the boundaries between the natural landscape and built structure.
